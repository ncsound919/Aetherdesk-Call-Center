# AetherDesk Knowledge Base

## Billing FAQ

### Invoice Questions
- To check your invoice status, please provide your invoice ID (format: ABC-12345)
- Invoices are typically sent within 24 hours of purchase
- You can view all your invoices on our customer portal

### Refund Policy
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- Original payment method determines refund timeline
- Contact billing for disputes over $500

## Pharmacy FAQ

### Prescription Refills
- Prescription refills require your prescription number (Rx number)
- Refills are ready for pickup in 2-4 hours
- We can mail prescriptions to your address on file

### Doctor Callback
- Doctor callbacks are available for medication questions
- Expect a call within 24 business hours
- Have your prescription number ready

## Order Status

### Tracking
- Order status requires your order ID and shipping ZIP code
- Orders ship within 1-2 business days
- Tracking info sent via email

## Technical Support

### Password Reset
- Password reset requires customer ID verification
- Temporary password sent to registered email
- Change password within 24 hours
